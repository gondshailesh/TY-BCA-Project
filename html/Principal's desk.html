Principal's Desk
Prof. Shirish L. Kotgire.Principal

We are living in the 21st century, the century of applied sciences and technology. To choose a career is itself a challenging task before young students and their parents aspiring for the success in life. Success is what everyone wants to achieve and all the successful people do not do different things, they do things differently. We are here to guide you.

The speed of development is very high and one cannot keep him in pace with the development without getting trained in one of the professional courses. The huge demand of trained professionals cannot be fulfilled with conventional courses and existing institutes. Diversified courses like B.Sc. Computer Science, B.C.A., M.Sc. Software Engineering, M.Sc. Electronics, B.Sc. Biotechnology, M.Sc. Biotechnology, B.Sc. Bioinformatics, M.Sc. Bioinformatics, M.Sc. Biochemistry, M.Sc. Genetics and M.Sc. Astrophysics have been designed to meet the need of time.

MGM's Institutes are known for their quality education with the best possible infrastructure, environment and discipline giving opportunities for all round development of personalities. Even the most brilliant students need the best of teaching, up to date technology, equipments and an enabling environment that encourages academic excellence! Our faculty not only teaches the scheduled curriculum but they mentor you into successful professionals.

MGM has maintained a long tradition of highly successful professionals coming out of the Engineering, Medical, and Management disciplines. The Computer, Electronics, Biotechnology and Bioinformatics professionals coming out of this institute will be the additional feathers in the cap that carries the pride of giving the bright professionals to the society.
