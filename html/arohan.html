Arohan

Arohan is a welcome party for new entrants in MGM College. It gives and greats plesures to welcomes our newely admitted students with a great enjoyments. So every year our college organizes fresher's party. i.e Arohan. In this party student give various performance like solo & group dance , solo & group signing etc. In this party MGM conducts Mr. Fresher's & Miss Fresher's competition in which students are tested on the basis of their talents & excellence in different contests like introduction , question -answer, also the same competition in which Mr. Abdul Aatif Latif [F.Y.
